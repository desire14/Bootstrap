About the Holy City's Sandwich Shop!

We are specialized in delicious, well prepared sandwiches that are the best in the Low Country. Our cold cuts meats are of the highest quality shipped daily from our distributor on John's Island. We start each day baking our daily requirement of fresh baked bread for our sandwiches.

Holy City's Sandwich Shop sandwiches are made with high quality meats, served on delicious San Francisco style breads freshly baked, we offer a choice of five different cheeses and also include "everything" (mayo, mustard, our special recipe garlic sauce, lettuce, tomato, pickles, peppers, onions) option. There are a variety of delicious sandwich combinations that have become tried and true favorites, or build your own. We also offer a variety of freshly made salads.

When our customers walk in and smell the fresh baked bread, have a big bite of their 'made to order' sandwich made with our savory garlic sauce, and are handed a warm cookie to complete their meal - how can they not LOVE Holy City's Sandwiches

Ingredients

Our oregno comes from Southern Italy where it is grown on a small farm and harvested lovingly by a 90 year old women, placing each piece in a straw basket prior to shipping to America

Our olive oil comes from Southern Italy where it is grown on a small farm and cold pressed prior to shipping to America

Our sea salt comes from Southern Italy where it is made by drawing sea water and after drying it is safely packaged prior to shipping to America

Our black pepper locally grown right here in the lowcountry.
